Burger

Description
A burger, or hamburger, is a sandwich made of a patty of ground meat, usually beef, between two slices of bread or a bun.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef patty (100% beef, seasoned with salt and pepper)
  • Burger bun (toasted, with sesame seeds or plain)
  • Cheddar cheese (optional, for a cheeseburger)
  • Lettuce (fresh and crisp)
  • Tomato slices (juicy and ripe)
  • Onions (raw or grilled, depending on preference)
  • Pickles (optional, for tangy flavor)
  • Ketchup (classic topping)
  • Mustard (optional)
  • Mayonnaise (optional, for added creaminess)
  • Bacon (optional, for extra flavor)
  • Special sauce (optional, for a signature touch)

Steps

  1. Shape the beef patty.
  2. Cook the patty.
  3. Toast the buns.
  4. Assemble the burger ; Layer lettuce, tomato, and pickles on the bottom bun. Add the patty with cheese, then onions and condiments. Top with the bun.
  5. Serve hot with your favorite sides!
