Mochi Ice Cream

Description
Mochi ice cream is a Japanese dessert that combines a soft, chewy, pounded rice dumpling (mochi) with an ice cream filling

Ingredients

  • Milk.
  • Cream.
  • Cane Sugar.
  • Whey.
  • Nonfat Dry Milk.
  • Caramel Color.
  • Stabilizer (Guar Gum, Locust Bean Gum).
  • Coffee.
  • Natural Flavors.

Steps

  1. Scoop small balls of ice cream and freeze until firm.
  2. Mix mochiko, water, and sugar. Microwave in intervals (1 min, stir, 1 min, stir, 30 sec) until thick and sticky.
